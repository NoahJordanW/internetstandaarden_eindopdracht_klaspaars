Games

On this page I will be going over my favorite games from the Dragon ball Z franchise. It would be absurd to cover every single game released because the franchise has released over 40 games spanning over the past 40 years.

Dragon Ball FighterZ

The first game on the list is Dragon Ball FighterZ. It is a 2D fighting game developed by Arc System Works.

It was released in 2018 for: Playstation 4, Nintendo Switch, Xbox ONE and Microsoft Windows

Dragon Ball Z Budokai Tenkaichi 3

The second game on the list is Dragon Ball Z Budokai Tenkaichi 3, it's a 3D arena style fighting game developed by Spike and released by bandai namco.

It was released in 2007 for: Playstation 2, Nintendo Wii

Dragon ball Z Legacy of Goku

The third game on the list is Dragon Ball Z Legacy of Goku. It's an adventure game that came out on the Nintendo Gameboy Advance in the year 2002 It was developed by Webfoot Technologies

Dragon ball Z Supersonic Warriors 2

Supersonic Warriors 2 is a 2D fighting game where the player chooses a team of three to fight against an enemy or another team. it was released in 2005 and developed by Cavia
