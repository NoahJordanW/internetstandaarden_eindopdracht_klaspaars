Dragon Ball Z: The Ultimate Anime and Gaming Series

Overview

Dragon ball Z is a series created by Akira Toriyama in 1986. Over the years the series has released 4 anime series, 20 movies and over 40 games. The series focusses on the protagonist named Goku. He is a Saiyan born on Planet Vegeta, send to earth when he was young. The story follows Goku on multiple adventures on earth and later throughout the galaxy saving planets and his friends. His goal is to become the strongest being in all of the universes and make friends along the way.
